# Zelda 2 Carrock Manipulation

Carrock can be manipulated to spawn in a limited number of patterns. The current best manipulation for damageless results in 4 possible patterns.

Start at Carrock, go out to the left, stab, right; then left, stab, right; then left, right; then left, right and walk straight to Carrock.

Instead of the usual fight, cast jump. Jump straight over Carrocks 1st position, and count to his 11th spawn, jump so that you avoid that 11th spawn.
